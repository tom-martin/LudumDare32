Zombie Splash City

A zombie infestation has taken over the city. Only you, the city street cleaner has escaped infection! You must find all the antidote and spray it on the zombies to cure them.

Instructions

  • Use WSAD to walk
  • Use the mouse to aim your water gun.
  • There are 3 antidote crates to collect.
  • Follow the red arrow to find the antidote crates.
